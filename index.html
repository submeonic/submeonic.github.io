ABOUT ME

Hello! I am an immersive developer and a technical creative. I often refer to myself as a tinkerer as I love to experiment with technology to invent new ways of interacting and experiencing. My ability to swiftly adapt to new pipelines and mediums is a cornerstone of my skill set. When I’m not immersed in technology, I’m unwinding by playing instruments and crafting soundtracks.

My educational background is in Games, Interactive Media and Mobile Technologies (GIMM) at Boise State University. The GIMM program has trained me to be a forward-thinker by exposing me to many different development pathways. What I like most about GIMM is the freedom it offers for creative exploration and goal setting, laying a robust foundation in high-level design, project scoping, and team management. This experience has been pivotal in shaping my approach to problem-solving and design thinking.

Professionally, I’ve delved into research development and high-level design where I’ve sharpened my hard skills in programming and tool usage. I aspire to be great at what I do and am constantly seeking opportunities to contribute to cool projects with cool people.
