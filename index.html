ABOUT ME

Hello! I am an immersive developer and technical creative, passionate about R&D in immersive technologies and pursuing graduate studies pertaining to interactive media technologies. My focus is on exploring technical solutions in experience design and spatial interactives, with a strong interest in graphics, spatial development, and real-time AI programming. I consider myself a tinkerer, always experimenting with technology to invent new ways of interacting and experiencing. When I’m not immersed in technology, I’m unwinding by playing instruments and crafting soundtracks.

My education in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technologies (GIMM) at Boise State University has trained me to adapt quickly to new pipelines and mediums, laying a foundation in high-level design, project scoping, and team management. This experience has shaped my approach to problem-solving and design thinking. Professionally, I have sharpened my programming and tool usage through research development and creative exploration. I am always seeking opportunities to collaborate on innovative projects.
